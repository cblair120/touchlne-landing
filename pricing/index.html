Plans & Pricing

Simple, scalable plans designed for clubs and organisations of all sizes.

Our plans are designed to fit you, not the other war round. Our flexible pricing options help provide our modernised managment system at an accessible price. TouchLNE has two versions, TouchLNE Club and TouchLNE Organisation. Our club plan allows easy team management, along with rostering and events. If your organisation is operating at the Pro tier or above, clubs will gain free access to TouchLNE Pro, and you do not need to do anything here. This access may be partially restricted by the organisation. If your organisation does not use TouchLNE, is working at the Basic tier, or you aren't associated with an organisation, then TouchLNE club is for you. TouchLNE Organisation provides full access for organisations and governing bodies to easily manage their competetion. This is the soloution for everything, providing many advanced features for a range of situations.
